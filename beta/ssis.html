SQLServer Integration Services (SSIS)

The ability to extract data from various sources and transforming it into meaningful information is essential to ensure competitiveness. Helping IT departments to analyse accurate data is critical to the decision making within an enterprise.

Our highly experienced BI (Business Intelligence) Development team has the necessary experience, skills and expertise to offer a solution as per requirements and challenges.

Our Competencies

We provide a range of services for SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) with the support of our highly experienced Business Intelligence Architects.

Some of the services provided by us from our engineering centers in India are given below:

  • Complete Requirement Gathering and Planning
  • Rich exposure in working with large volumes of non-structured data (web logs, blog posts, documents, web site content etc.)
  • Datawarehouse loading services
  • As an offshore SSIS development company, we can capture real-time data from sensors and other industrial gadgets, analyze the data streams in-memory and gain insights on-the-fly (experienced in working with Microsoft StreamInsight)
  • Extract data from Microsoft and non-Microsoft data sources including SQL Server, Oracle, Teradata, text and XML/CSV files, ODBC and OLE DB sources
  • Integrate ETL packages with on-premise file systems, FTP sites and cloud storages
  • Extract data from workflow engines like BizTalk, K2 BlackPearl, and Windows Workflow Foundation etc.
  • Data and Text Mining
  • Maintenance and support Service
  • Data transfer services
  • Experienced in Vast data normalization and cleansing standardization
  • Highly knowledgeable in loading transformed data into relational databases and dimensional data warehouses.
