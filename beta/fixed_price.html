Fixed Price Approach

This option involves fixed price approach to development based on detailed specification and would be ideally suitable for situations where you want to outsource a complete project/product development for a fixed price.

Development projects are broken down into logical deliverables based on time-box or functionality or based on specific go-to-market strategies and milestones. Ideally suitable for projects where requirements and deliverables are clearly defined. If detailed specification is not available, we can assist with a feasibility study and develop a detailed specification. You can take full leverage of Idyllic Tech Development Project Management and Business Analysis services.

Features

  • Based on the Business Requirements Documentation (BRD) Idyllic Tech would write the complete technical specification and a proposal would be submitted for your sign off.
  • The project would be managed by an experienced Project Manager who would be responsible for the end-to-end management of the project requirements, design, development, testing and deployment.
  • Project would be executed on a firm fixed-price basis.
  • Project deliverables would be incremental and would based on various milestones/activities agreed with the customer.
  • Complete support during customer User Acceptance Testing (UAT), deployment.
  • Well defined change control management procedures to take care of any changes suggested during the course of the project.
  • Includes post-implementation warranty of deliverables for an agreed period of time to fix any bugs not identified during UAT phase.
  • We retain core project staff to provide ongoing support and maintenance, if needed.
  • We strongly recommend that customers consider this as it helps customer to retain project resources who have knowledge and know-how of the application domain and system.

Advantages

  • Fully outsourced, so no hassles of employee management, resource availability and other issues.
  • Due to the incremental delivery approach, customers would have access to the deliverables at every stage of the project.
  • Project Managed by experienced project managers and delivery managers – due to our vast experience successfully managing fixed price projects, you are assured of Complete Reliability, Responsiveness and utmost Peacefulness.
